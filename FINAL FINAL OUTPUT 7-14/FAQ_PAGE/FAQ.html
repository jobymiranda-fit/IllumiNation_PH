Frequently Asked Questions

Your guide to understanding our mission and how you can help.

What is the main goal of IllumiNation PH?

IllumiNation PH is an organization committed to bringing light and electricity to underserved communities across the Philippines. Our ultimate goal is to empower these communities, fostering better educational opportunities, sustainable community development, and environmental conservation through clean energy.

How can I help or get involved?

There are several impactful ways to get involved! You can make a financial contribution on our 'Donate' page or sign up for hands-on work through our 'Volunteer' page. Simply spreading the word about our cause also makes a huge difference.

Where does my donation go?

100% of your donation goes directly toward our core programs. This includes funding educational initiatives, supporting the development of sustainable communities, and aiding our environmental conservation efforts in the areas we serve.

What is the purpose of the Interactive Map?

The 'Interactive Map' is a feature on our website that visually showcases our impact. It allows you to explore the locations of our current and completed projects, see which communities we are actively supporting, and find information about upcoming events and activities.

Are there specific upcoming activities I can join?

Yes! We have several activities planned. On our homepage, you can see cards for upcoming events, including their date and location. For more details on how to join, please visit the 'Volunteer' section of our website.

Who are the people behind this organization?

Our organization is driven by a dedicated group of professionals, including a Project Manager, Developer, Quality Analyst, and a Data Management Lead. You can learn more about the passionate individuals on our team in the "Meet Our Team" section on the homepage.
